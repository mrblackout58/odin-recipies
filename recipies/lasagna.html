Lasagna

Description:

An easy and simple lasagna recipie that saves time while still being delicious

Ingredience

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 (32-ounce) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesian cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Ingredience

  1. Gather all ingredience and preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until brown and crumbly, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated parmesian cheese, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl
  4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with 3 uncookedlasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup of sauce; repeat layers once more.
  5. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesian cheese. Pour 1/2 cup of water along the edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
