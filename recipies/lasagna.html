Lasagna

Description:

An easy and simple lasagna recipie that saves time while still being delicious

Ingredience

  • Beef: Ground beef or turkey works
  • Cheeses: Cottage cheese, mozzarella and parmesian
  • Spaghetti sauce: Homemade preferred but store bought works
  • Seasonings: Season with dried parsley, salt and black pepper
  • Eggs: Helps bind the mixture of cheeses while also lending moisture
  • Lasagna Noodles: Obviously noodles are needed
  • Water: Pour 1/2 of a cup

Ingredience

  1. Cook beef, then drain
  2. Stir in spaghetti sauce and let simmer
  3. Mix cottage cheese with 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of parmesian, and seasonings
  4. Assemble lasagna according to the detailed recipie
  5. Bake covered for 45 minutes
  6. Uncover and bake for 10 more minutes
